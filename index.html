About

Hello! My name is Mar and I'm a UX/UI Designer. I'm empathetic, curious, hard worker and I especially have a great desire to start working as a UX/UI Designer and contribute with my knowledge and values. I love improving people's lives, by looking for the problems first and then finding relevant solutions, all through different research methods.

My goal is to work in a company with clear proposals and values, and an amazing UX/UI team where I can keep learning and training my skills. Also, I want to be able to contribute with my knowledge, skills, and attitude. In short, with everything I can.

I discovered UX/UI Design less than a year ago. I began to be interested in graphic design but I knew it wasn't exactly what I wanted. For me, the research part was missing from the graphic design process, and so was knowing the reason for everything and for every decision. This was different in UX/UI, finally, I found in this discipline all I was looking for.

I love the research side, which includes a human and social part, which is so necessary for me. The creation and design aspects, that immediately attracted me and made me want to learn more.

I have been able to apply my background in Science, especially in the UX Design part, because I had knowledge of research and interpretation of results as well as of definition of hypotheses and objectives.

If you are interested in getting to know more about me, don't hesitate to contact me.
