About

Hi! I'm Mar and I work as a UX/UI Designer. I'm empathetic, curious and hard worker. I love improving people's lives, by looking for the problems first and then finding relevant solutions, all through different research methods.

I'm working in the UX/UI Design industry for 1 year now and I can affirm that this is just the beginning of a long career that I am improving day by day to be a better designer and professional.

Curiosity for research, search and find the best solutions based on the interests of users and companies. I like to work hand in hand with the Developers and Tech Team, I think it's very useful to create a better product.

I'm always open to listen and be part of new challenges and projects so, if you are interested in getting to know more about me or you think that I can fit in your project, don't hesitate to get in touch with me.
